FAQ

How do you make Free Picks?

We analyze recent official matches (typically the last 10–15 per team) to estimate Over/Under probabilities. We show the two most confident Over/Under 2.5 selections each day.

What if there are no top-league matches today?

We first search Top-5 European leagues and UEFA competitions. If we can’t find two confident picks, we label alternatives from reliable fallback leagues (e.g., Eredivisie, Portugal, Belgium, MLS).

What’s the Hero Bet of the Day?

A single value pick (odds ≥ 2.00) selected from Over/Under, BTTS, Corners, Cards, or 1X2 based on volatile but favorable stats.

How accurate are the picks?

Accuracy varies by day and season. We display confidence % and reasoning for every pick so you can judge the call. We’ll add a “Last 30 days” tracker to show transparency over time.

How do I get Pro access?

Choose a plan on the Pricing page. After checkout, your account unlocks the Pro Board and Hero Bet.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time.
