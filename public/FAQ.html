Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about signing in, subscriptions, Free Picks, Pro Board, and Hero Bet.

How do I sign in?
  1. Click Sign in (top-right).
  2. Choose your Google account and complete the popup.
  3. You’ll see your email appear in the header when signed in.
How do I get Pro access?
  1. Sign in (top-right).
  2. Go to Pricing and pick Weekly / Monthly / Quarterly / Yearly.
  3. Complete Stripe Checkout. You’ll return to Pro.

Use the same email you signed in with for the smoothest experience.

I subscribed but Pro still says “No active subscription”. What should I do?
  • Make sure you’re signed in with the same email you used at checkout.
  • Refresh the page (or sign out/in) and try again.
  • It can take a minute for the payment to reflect.
  • If it persists, email overedgestats@gmail.com with your Stripe receipt email.
What is the Pro Board?

The Pro Board is a ranked slate of today’s fixtures from our allowed leagues. Each row shows the match, kickoff, and quick “Top Bets” (OU 2.5, BTTS, 1X2, Cards, Corners) with a confidence %.

What is the Hero Bet?

One featured pick from tier-1 leagues with our strongest calibrated edge, including market, prediction, confidence %, and a short reason (ExpG + goal rates).

Why are there no Free Picks today?
  • The day may have a light slate or fixtures that don’t meet our thresholds.
  • Odds can be missing for some lower-tier games; if strict filtering is on, picks may be withheld.
  • Try again later, or check another date on the Free Picks page.
Which leagues do you cover?

We focus on European domestic leagues (youth/women/reserve are filtered). The Pro Board uses a curated allow-list; Free Picks avoid those to keep products distinct. Hero Bet looks at a smaller tier-1 set.

What timezone are kickoffs shown in?

Kickoff times display in your browser’s local time. If you query dates on the API directly, those dates are in UTC.

Do you guarantee profit or outcomes?

No—OverEdge provides informational analysis only. Always bet responsibly and follow your local laws.

How do I cancel or manage billing?

If you need help with changes, just email overedgestats@gmail.com from your account email and we’ll assist promptly.
